About Me

Hello! My name is Milly Trejo. I am a graduate of the University of North Alabama with a Bachelor of Business Administration in Computer Information Systems. My academic journey began at Bevill State Community College, where I earned an Associate of Science in General Studies, became an active member of Phi Theta Kappa, and received both an academic business award and the UNA Scholarship of Distinction in Computing.

During my time at UNA, I developed a passion for technology and its rapid advancements. Through academic and personal projects, I focused on creating innovative and practical solutions while exploring ways to combine creativity with technical problem-solving. I am eager to grow in a role where I can contribute to impactful projects, continue expanding my skills, and help drive innovation.
